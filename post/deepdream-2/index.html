Deep Dream does things

The importance of GPU

After my first article about Google’s Deep Dreams, I’ve kept trying new configurations and more photos, so I just wanted to share some of the results that I like the most.

An important note is that I’ve been using cuda and a relatively powerful GPU (an NVIDIA Quadro K4000), so generating these images for me was a matter of seconds. If you have a less powerful GPU, it may take more time to achieve meaningful results; if you are completely lacking a GPU, expect each iteration to take 3 to 10 minutes.
